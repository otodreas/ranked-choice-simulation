# Ranked choice voting in maine demo
Votes tabulated in rounds until only 2 candidates remain.

## How it works
You can choose only 1 candidate, or choose to rank all candidates, or everything between.

### Round 1
First choice candidates are tabulated on election night. If a candidate gets the majority of votes, they win.

### Further rounds
If no one wins round 1, round 2 starts.
- Candidate with the fewest votes is eliminated
- People who voted for the candidate with fewest votes as first choice have their second place candidate cast as their vote in the next round
    - If the second place candidate is no longer in the race, they vote for their third choice candidate, and so on. If they didn't rank anyone else (they've only cast votes for eliminated candidates), their ballot is eliminated.
- Votes in the next round are tallied, and the candidate winning the fewest votes gets eliminated
